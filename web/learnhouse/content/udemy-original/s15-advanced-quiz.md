## 15 Bonus Advanced Quiz

### What it is?

The **15 Bonus Advanced Quiz** covers **double POI**, **BOS bounce**, **market cycle**, **momentum**, **signal power (IB + IMB)**, **set & forget vs confirmation**, **both-side POI**, and **live session references** (weak 3R vs strong 6R). Pass before **S17 final exam** with ≥12/15; Jayce targets **15/15**.

**Advanced** means **process scenarios** — choose document/skip/wait/confirmation, not "pick winning trade for profit guarantee."
