## My two orders on Sep 29 - Scalping with BOS bounce and double POI

### What it is?

This lesson closes the **live week Sep 26–29** with **two separate scalping orders** on **September 29**, both executed using the **main course strategy stack**:

1. **Double POI** (S03) — HTF POI #1 + LTF refined POI #2 for stop placement and RR.
2. **BOS bounce** (S04) — intraday confirmation trigger on M5/M15 after POI tap.

Jayce titles this section **"scalping trade explanation"** because he **slows the replay** — narrating each phase of the footprint, not only entry and exit screenshots. This is the bridge between **live PnL sessions (S08–S10)** and **journal deep dives (S12)**.

**Two orders meaning:** Not necessarily both winners — the pedagogy is **two independent setups** same day (often **US30 + XAUUSD**, or two US30 legs morning/afternoon). Typical pattern from course narrative:

- **Order A:** Follow-trend pullback to double POI + BOS bounce long.
- **Order B:** Second session setup — continuation or counter-scalp after new liquidity event.

**Sep 29 context:** After **Sep 26 four-trade day**, **Sep 27 weak 3R**, **Sep 28 strong 6R**, Sep 29 returns to **operator-normal** — power scores **17–21**, targets **1.5R–3R** each, combined day **+2% to +4% account** in journal cross-references.

**Why double POI + BOS bounce together:** Double POI answers **where** on chart; BOS bounce answers **when** to click. Without POI, BOS is noise. Without BOS on medium power, POI first touch is **Sep 26 Trade #1 loss pattern**.

### How to define or apply it?

**Unified checklist for both Sep 29 orders:**

**Phase A — HTF markup (before session):**

1. D1 + H4 bias per instrument.
2. Mark premium/discount — entries only in correct half for direction.
3. Identify **liquidity pools** for the session (Asia high/low, equal highs/lows).

**Phase B — Double POI (S03):**

4. After sweep + displacement, mark **POI #1** (H1/H4 OB).
5. Mark **POI #2** (M15 nested OB or FVG CE inside POI #1).
6. Invalidation: below sweep (long) / above sweep (short).

**Phase C — BOS bounce (S04):**

7. Price trades into POI #2 (or #1 if shallow pullback).
8. Wait **rejection wick** — do not front-run.
9. M5 **body close** breaks last minor swing in trade direction = BOS bounce.
10. Enter on retest of broken structure or market after close — Jayce shows both.

**Phase D — Scalp management:**

11. Score power (S07) — Order A and B logged separately.
12. Risk **1% each** — two orders ≠ 2% simultaneous if correlated; Jayce notes **correlation risk** when both US30 and NAS long.
13. TP1 liquidity / 1.5R–2R; TP2 optional if momentum holds.
14. **Max 2–3 scalps** Sep 29 style day unless new H4 structure.

**Order-specific nuances:**

| Order | Typical setup | Power band |
|-------|---------------|------------|
| #1 Morning | Fresh sweep + double POI | 18–22 |
| #2 Afternoon | Pullback in trend or new sweep | 15–20 |
