## Scalping and get 3R with Smart Money Concepts in weak case

### What it is?

This lesson is Jayce's **September 27 live trade** — deliberately labeled **"weak case"** in the Udemy title to contrast with **Sep 28 strongest case (6R)** in S10. **Weak** does **not** mean random or illegal gamble — it means **signal power below "strongest" tier** (S07 total IB+IMB typically **12–16**, momentum **15–18**) yet the **footprint was complete enough** to justify a **small, confirmation-only scalp** with excellent **risk-reward to nearest liquidity**.

**3R meaning:** Profit = **three times risk** on the trade — if you risked 1% account, a full 3R win ≈ **+3% account** before partials. Jayce emphasizes **R journal** over $ PnL so prop firm traders normalize across account sizes.

**Why teach weak cases:** Students wait for "perfect 25/25 power" charts that appear **twice a week**. Real income comes from **B+ setups** with **strict gates** — half size, confirmation entry, fixed TP at 3R liquidity — not from skipping everything below 20 power.

**Instrument / session:** Replay focuses **US30 or XAUUSD** during London–NY; chart shows **messier pullback** — FVG partially filled, OB less clean than Sep 28. **Weak case hallmarks:**

- IB score **8–11** (OB valid but not nested institutional base)
- IMB score **4–6** (gap partially mitigated)
- Momentum **16–18** (impulse slowing)
- **Premium/discount** correct side but price **mid-range** briefly — over-zone risk (S05)

**Still tradable because:** Liquidity sweep + manipulation + POI + **BOS bounce** aligned; **HTF bias** not contradicted; **invalidation** tight and obvious.

### How to define or apply it?

**Weak case trading rules (Jayce mandatory):**

| Rule | Strong case (S10) | Weak case (this lesson) |
|------|-------------------|-------------------------|
| Power total | ≥20 | 12–16 only |
| Entry | Set & forget optional | **Confirmation only** |
| Size | 1–1.5% risk | **0.75–1% risk** |
| TP style | Partials + runner | **Fixed 2R–3R** primary |
| Skip if | — | Both-side POI, news spike, no sweep |

**Step-by-step application:**

1. **Morning prep:** Mark D1/H4 bias — Sep 27 example: cautious bullish on US30 with **D1 resistance nearby** → scalp only, no swing hold.
2. **Wait for sweep** — no trade in middle of range (weak case trap #1).
3. **Score power honestly** — if total **<12**, **no trade** (this day was ~14–15 on Jayce replay).
4. **Mark POI** — single OB acceptable; double POI ideal but not required in weak case if FVG CE aligns.
5. **Do not limit at first touch** — require **M5 BOS bounce** (S04).
6. **Stop** beyond sweep — accept wider stop in points but **tight in structure**; lot size from S19 calc.
7. **TP at 3R** or first major liquidity — **do not** hope for 6R in weak case.
8. **Journal tag:** `WEAK_CASE_3R` + power breakdown.

**Psychology:** Weak case wins build **confidence without fantasy** — you learn that SMC is **probability with rules**, not only "institutional perfect" photos.

**Common mistakes on weak days:**

- Labeling weak chart **strong** to justify set & forget — **OVERSTATED_IB** tag
- Moving TP to 6R because Sep 28 video — **greed**
- Trading weak case **after** two losses same day — **skip**
