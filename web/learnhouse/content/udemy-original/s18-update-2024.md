## How to use Smart Money correctly? Real story about Fair value gap or Imbalance

### What it is?

**2024 update** — Jayce addresses **misuse of FVG/imbalance** across social media: traders fading every gap, trading **tiny M1 FVGs**, or assuming **fill = reversal**. This lesson is a **real story** walkthrough — wins and losses from **imbalance-only** trading vs **full footprint** trading.

**Core correction:**

- FVG is **not** entry signal alone — needs **liquidity sweep**, **POI**, **bias**, often **BOS bounce**.
- **Fill ≠ fade** — price can fill gap and **continue** (trend continuation rebalance).
- **News gaps** behave differently from **structural displacement** gaps.
- **S19 indicator** highlights gaps — **manual power score** still rules (S07).

**Real story arc (course narrative):**

1. Student trades **every** highlighted FVG — win rate looks OK short sample, **blows account** on continuation fills.
2. Jayce adds **IMB spent** journal column — fully filled FVG before POI = downgrade (S07).
3. Same student adopts **footprint checklist** — fewer trades, better R.

**Link Sep 28 strongest case:** Stacked **unfilled** FVG + POI = high IMB score — contrast weak Sep 27 partial fill.

### How to define or apply it?

**2024 IMB rules (add to journal):**

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| FVG unfilled, POI overlap | High IMB score — eligible per power total |
| FVG 50% CE hold | Medium-high |
| FVG fully filled before POI test | **IMB spent** — confirmation only or skip |
| M1 micro FVG | Ignore |
| News spike FVG first hour | Skip or wait structure |

**Correct SMC sequence (unchanged):**

Liquidity → manipulation → displacement (creates FVG) → POI → entry trigger.

**Never:**

- "FVG fill short" without bearish narrative
- Remove stop because "imbalance must hold"

**XAUUSD 2024 replay panel**

1. **Wrong trade:** Short at FVG fill in **H4 markup** — stopped on continuation. Journal tag: `IMB_ONLY_ERROR`.
2. **Right trade:** Same day later — sweep highs, bearish POI at premium, unfilled FVG overlap, BOS down — short to liquidity.

**Student drill:** Mark 10 FVG fills — classify continuation vs reversal using **HTF bias** — calibrate eyes.

## Market structure rules: Explain and compare TF and TF/2 standard

### What it is?

Second **2024 update** — formalizes **multi-timeframe structure rules** Jayce teased in S07:

- **TF standard:** Use **same timeframe family** for bias and execution (e.g., H4 bias, H1 POI, M15 entry).
- **TF/2 standard:** Use **half timeframe** for entry relative to bias TF (e.g., H4 bias → H2 or H1 entry refinement; H1 bias → M30/M15 entry).

**Purpose:** When LTF conflicts HTF, **do not** take LTF counter-trend as full trade — use **HTF for bias**, **TF/2 for precision entry** in HTF direction only.

**Compare:**

| Model | Bias TF | Structure/POI | Execution |
|-------|---------|---------------|-----------|
| TF | H4 | H4/H1 | M15/M5 |
| TF/2 | H4 | H2/H1 | M15/M5 aligned |

**Conflict rule:** M5 bullish BOS but H4 bearish POI at premium — **no long**; wait H4 narrative or sit out (both-side S07).

### How to define or apply it?

**Weekly markup workflow:**

1. Set **bias TF** = D1 + H4 (always).
2. Mark POI on **bias TF or one step down** (H4 or H1).
3. Set **execution TF** = TF/2 of POI TF (H1 POI → M15/M5 triggers).
4. If execution signal **against** bias TF CHoCH — **skip**.

**Journal columns:** `Bias TF`, `POI TF`, `Entry TF` — must be documented line.

**Prop firm scalping:** US30 often H4 bias, M15 POI, M5 BOS — fits TF/2 spirit.

### Example

**US30 long 2024**

- H4 bullish BOS — bias long.
- POI on H1 — TF/2 execution on M15 BOS bounce.
- M5 bearish CHoCH while H4 intact — **pullback only**, not flip short unless H4 invalidates.
