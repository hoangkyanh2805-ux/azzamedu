## 7 Levels to be professional with Smart Money Concepts - Practice guidance

### What it is?

Jayce maps the **NCI Trading school ladder** — **7 levels** from complete beginner to professional SMC operator. This bonus lesson is **practice guidance**, not a promise of profitability: it tells you **what to master in order** before adding complexity. Level 2 on Udemy is **this SMC course**; Levels 0–1 are prerequisites; Levels 3–5 are **advanced paths** (combine strategies, day trading, investing).

**The 7 levels (Jayce framework):**

| Level | Name | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| 0 | Multi-timeframe (MTF) | Read H4/D1 without trading |
| 1 | Key level | HTF support/resistance anchors (NCI Level 1) |
| 2 | Smart Money Concepts | This course — definitions + double POI + BOS |
| 3 | Combine | Wyckoff + Elliott + SMC integration |
| 4 | Day trading | Scalping specialization, session rules |
| 5 | Investing | Swing hold, macro bias |
| 6–7 | Professional / mentor | Journal review, prop firm, teaching |

**Practice guidance** = weekly drills per level — backtest counts, quiz scores, journal completeness gates before advancing.

**Why 7 levels:** Prevents **strategy hopping** — students who skip Level 0–1 mark POIs in vacuum (EURCAD lesson S06 needs key levels).

### How to define or apply it?

**Level 0 drill (2 weeks):**

- Daily: mark D1/H4 swing high/low on 3 pairs — **no orders**.
- Pass: explain bias in writing 10 days straight.

**Level 1 drill (2 weeks):**

- Mark **3 key levels** per pair from Level 1 course.
- Pass: 5 historical bounces documented.

**Level 2 drill (this Udemy course — 1–3 months):**

- Complete S02–S07 + S14–S15 quizzes **100%**.
- Backtest **50** double POI setups on demo — journal all.
- Pass: **30 live demo trades** with checklist compliance ≥85%.

**Level 3+ (after Level 2 pass):**

- Only then add combine course / VIP — Jayce warns against buying everything day one.

**Weekly practice schedule (template):**

| Day | Activity |
|-----|----------|
| Mon | Definitions replay + 10 chart marks |
| Tue | Backtest 3 setups |
| Wed | Quiz retake weak areas |
| Thu | Live demo max 2 trades |
| Fri | Journal export + one fix |

### Example

**Student path — "Yusuf" style (S01 reference):**

- Month 1: Level 0–1 + S02 definitions.
- Month 2: S03–S06 backtest only.
- Month 3: S08–S12 journal + demo scalps — withdrawal story is **individual**, not typical.

**Jayce Q&A:** "Which level am I?" — If you cannot score signal power on cold chart, you are **Level 2 mid**, not Level 4.

## Start learning & practicing from Zero to Profitable level

### What it is?

The **zero-to-practice** roadmap — Jayce's answer to "I am new, where do I click?" **Profitable** in the title means **structured toward positive expectancy**, not **guaranteed income**. Sequence: **education → demo proof → small live → scale** with prop firm optional.

**Zero** = no SMC vocabulary yet. **Profitable level** = consistent **process metrics** (checklist compliance, R expectancy on journal) — dollars follow only after.

**Three pillars:**

1. **Learn** — Udemy sections in order; Resources PDFs.
2. **Practice** — 1–3 months demo minimum (S01 requirement).
3. **Prove** — journal sample Jayce can grade in Discord review.

**Anti-patterns at zero:**

- Live $100 account to "feel real" — too small, bad habits.
- Skip quizzes — fail final exam and real markets harder.
- Telegram signal copy — course spam warning S01.

### How to define or apply it?

**Month-by-month starter map:**

**Month 1 — Vocabulary**

- S01–S02 complete; S14 basic quiz until 100%.
- Daily 30 min chart marking liquidity only.

**Month 2 — Strategy skeleton**

- S03–S05; backtest 20 follow-trend POIs.
- S15 advanced quiz started.

**Month 3 — Examples + power**

- S06–S07; score every backtest trade IB/IMB.
- S08–S11 live replays on TradingView replay mode.

**Month 4 — Journal + demo execution**

- S12 template daily on demo.
- Max risk 0.5% until 20 trades logged.

**Month 5+ — live small or prop challenge**

- Risk 1%; S19 lot calculator; S16 mindset.

**Gate to live money:**

## Share LIQUIDITY indicator and how to use

### What it is?

Jayce shares the **NCI LIQUIDITY indicator** (TradingView Pine or MT4/5 equivalent in Resources) — a **helper tool** that highlights **equal highs/lows**, **swing liquidity pools**, and **recent sweep zones**. **Not** a buy/sell signal — same philosophy as S19 imbalance tool: **indicator finds, trader grades**.

**Why liquidity first among indicators:** Entire SMC footprint starts at **Phase 0 liquidity test** (S02). Students who mis-mark liquidity mis-mark everything downstream.

**Indicator features (typical):**

- Auto-detect equal highs/lows clusters
- Color sweep when wick breaks pool
- Optional session high/low lines

**Limitations Jayce stresses:**

- Repainting on **forming candle** — confirm on close.
- Crypto/settings sensitivity — tune lookback.
- Cannot replace **HTF bias** — only execution aid.

### How to define or apply it?

**Install (Resources tab):**

1. Download Pine script / MT file from Udemy Resources.
2. Apply to **US30 or XAUUSD** first — Jayce's teaching pairs.
3. Default settings — do not optimize until manual marking mastered.

**Workflow with SMC:**

1. Indicator flags **potential** liquidity — **you confirm** with S02 rules (equal highs, obvious swing).
2. After sweep, **disable** reliance on indicator — switch to POI/FVG manual markup.
3. Journal: `Liq indicator agree Y/N` — build trust over 20 days.

**Pair with S19:** Liquidity indicator + imbalance indicator = **find zones**; **signal power** still manual.

**Do not:**

- Enter because indicator drew arrow (if any — Jayce official script should not arrow).
- Use on M1 only — attach H1/H4 for context.

### Example

**US30 morning setup with indicator**

1. Indicator marks equal lows at **38500** zone (illustrative).
2. Jayce confirms visually — matches manual mark.
3. Sweep wick — indicator colors sweep; Jayce narrates manipulation.
4. Manual POI + BOS bounce entry — indicator **off** during decision to avoid distraction.
5. Journal notes agreement — builds habit of **eyes first, tool second**.

**Homework:** Run 10 historical days — log false positives where indicator marked liquidity but **no sweep** occurred — learn tool limits.
