## How to choose FOREX PAIR for scalping when favorite asset in bad trend

### What it is?

Jayce answers the **most common Q&A question:** your favorite pair (often **XAUUSD** or **EURUSD**) is in **chop or wrong-phase cycle** — do you force trades or switch? This tip lesson teaches **pair rotation for scalping**: keep **same SMC rules**, change **instrument** when HTF structure is **unclean** on favorite asset.

**Bad trend definition:**

- No clear D1/H4 BOS sequence — overlapping ranges.
- Repeated **both-side** POI failures (S07).
- **Over-zone** at HTF supply/demand — momentum dead (S05).
- Post-news whipsaw without settled structure.

**Scalping pair criteria (Jayce ranked):**

1. **Clean liquidity** — US30, XAUUSD, GBPUSD (session dependent).
2. **Volatility fit** — enough movement for 1.5R–3R intraday, not random spikes.
3. **HTF bias clarity** — one sentence bias possible.
4. **Spread/session** — London/NY overlap for forex; US30 NY.

**Do not:** Trade 10 pairs daily — **master 2–3** rotation list.

### How to define or apply it?

**Monday pair selection ritual (15 min):**

1. Score **favorite pair** HTF: `Clean Y/N`.
2. If **N**, scan rotation list: US30, NAS100, GBPUSD, XAUUSD — mark which has **sweep + bias** this week.
3. Pick **one index + one forex** max per day.
4. Journal header: `Primary pair` + `Backup pair`.

**When favorite returns:** Switch back when **D1 BOS** and cycle phase align — not because you miss gold emotionally.

**USDCHF lesson link:** Slow pair punishes lazy markup — not default scalp rotation (S06).

### Example

**Week example:** XAUUSD H4 distribution at highs — Jayce marks **no long bias** on gold. Rotates to **US30 markup** leg for Sep-style scalps. Student who forces gold longs loses — same rules, wrong battlefield.

**Journal field:** `Pair rotation reason` — one line.

## [Trading tips] Free TP or fixed TP when scalping?

### What it is?

**Free TP** = trail stop, hold for **nearest liquidity** or structure exit without fixed R cap — common on **strongest case** (Sep 28 6R). **Fixed TP** = pre-set **1.5R–3R** at defined level — common on **weak case** and **medium power** scalps.

Jayce does **not** say one is always better — **match TP style to power score and session time**.

| Style | When |
|-------|------|
| Fixed TP | Power <20, weak case, late session, pre-news |
| Free TP / runner | Power ≥22, clear liquidity ladder, morning momentum |

**Scalping misconception:** "Scalp = always fixed 10 pips" — wrong in SMC; scalp = **short hold**, TP can be **structural liquidity** far in points if stop is tight (high RR).

### How to define or apply it?

**Decision tree:**

1. Score power — if **<18** → fixed TP **2R max**.
2. If **18–21** → fixed TP at first major liquidity; optional **small runner** 25% size.
3. If **≥22** → partial fixed at 2R + runner to liquidity (free TP on runner).
4. Write TP plan **before entry** in journal — no mid-trade improvisation.

**Sep 26 Trade #3** = fixed ~1.5R. **Sep 28** = partial + free runner to 6R.

### Example

**US30 afternoon:** Power 17 — Jayce sets **fixed TP** at mid-range liquidity; price later runs further — **no regret**; plan was correct for score.

**Exercise:** 20 replay trades — tag fixed vs free; compare stress level and rule adherence.

## [Mindset] KEY MINDSET to set MONTHLY TARGET

### What it is?

Jayce reframes **monthly target** from **"make $10k"** to **process targets** you control:

- **% max loss** per day/week
- **Number of A-grade setups** taken
- **Journal compliance %**
- **Quiz / backtest milestones**

**Income target** can exist privately but must sit **after** process targets — otherwise traders overtrade last week of month to "hit number."

**Illustrative math (education, not promise):**

- **2% average net per week** on prop account with **1% risk/trade** ≈ steady growth if expectancy positive.
- **5%/day** (S12 headline) × 20 days = unrealistic expectation — **outlier days** only.

**KEY MINDSET:** Monthly success = **followed rules on losing weeks too**.

### How to define or apply it?

**Monthly card (write first of month):**

1. Max **red days** allowed: e.g., 8
2. Max daily loss: **3–4% account**
3. Target **A setups/week**: 5
4. One **skill focus**: e.g., "BOS bounce only, no POI #1 early"
5. Review date: last Sunday

**Prop firm:** Align with **drawdown rules** — monthly target must be **sub-max drawdown**.

### Example

**Student sets $5k month on $50k — Jayce converts:** Need **10% month** — requires either higher risk (danger) or **exceptional R** (rare). Revises to **4% month process** first — 2% week average.

## Understand this situation to change risk control mindset, not find entry point

### What it is?

Counter-intuitive tip: sometimes the **correct response** is **not** finding a better entry — it is **reducing risk or stopping**. Situations:

- **Losing streak** — edge intact but variance hurts; size down 50%.
- **Daily loss −2.5%** — next trade is **0.5% risk max** or stop.
- **Correlation overload** — US30 + NAS + gold all same direction unintentionally.
- **Life stress** — journal emotion score high — **no live**.

Jayce: "You cannot **entry-point** your way out of **risk control** failure."

### How to define or apply it?

**Risk control triggers (automatic):**

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| −2% day | One more trade max, half size |
| −3% day | **Stop** |
| 3 losses row | Stop 24h or demo only |
| Missed sleep | Demo or skip |

**Mindset shift:** Ask "**Should I trade?**" before "**Where do I enter?**"

### Example

**Friday after −2.8%:** Student finds "perfect" POI — Jayce says **walk** — saved from tilt loss. Monday fresh bias wins.

## MADE 1000k$ last week and LOST all this week, WHY? How to trade better?

### What it is?

**Boom/bust parable** — Jayce narrates student archetype (names anonymized): **large win week** on **oversized risk** or **lucky runner**, then **gives back** next week by **breaking rules** (no stops, revenge, pair hopping). Title uses **$1000k** phrasing from Udemy — interpret as **$1,000,000 narrative** or **typo-style $1000 week**; lesson applies to **any** boom/bust %.

**WHY happens:**

1. **Risk rose** after wins — implicit martingale.
2. **Setup quality dropped** — traded B setups at A size.
3. **No daily stop** — traded chop to "recover."
4. **Identity shift** — "I am genius" → skip checklist.

**How to trade better:** Same **1% risk** after win week; **journal mistake tags**; **weekly max loss** cap.

### How to define or apply it?

**After big win week:**

- Withdraw or move profits off table (psychological).
- **Reset** trade counter — next week = week 1 discipline.
- **No** new pairs or strategies for 2 weeks.

**Journal:** `Post-win sizing error Y/N` — review monthly.

### Example

**Archetype:** +30% week on one 6R runner with 3% risk — next week −25% on chop with 2% risk trades. Fix: runner allowed only at power ≥22; chop = **no trade**.

## How to RECOVER after LOSS and BUILD PSYCHOLOGY after a losing streak?

### What it is?

Recovery protocol after **loss** and **losing streak** — complements Sep 26 first trade −1.5% and USDCHF S06. **Recover** means **restore process**, not **recover money same session**.

**Phases:**

1. **Stop** — clock / day / week per rules.
2. **Journal RCA** — every loss tagged (S06).
3. **Demo replay** — 5 setups **no click**, markup only.
4. **Size down** — return live at **0.5% risk** for 10 trades.
5. **Scale** — back to 1% when compliance ≥90%.

**Psychology tools:**

- Pre-trade mantra: "I trade **plan**, not **PnL**."
- Physical break — walk 30 min (Sep 26 rule).
- Accountability post in Discord — **screenshot before**, not result brag.

### How to define or apply it?

**Losing streak (3+ days red):**

- **Mandatory** 48h demo or half size week.
- One **single fix** from RCA pivot — not 10 new rules.

**Never:**

- Double lot to recover
- Trade news to "get it back"
- Skip journal because painful

### Example

**After −8% month:** Student demos 2 weeks, fixes only `EARLY_POI` tag, returns with **3R weak cases** only — psychology rebuilds on **small wins + rules**, not one hero 6R.
