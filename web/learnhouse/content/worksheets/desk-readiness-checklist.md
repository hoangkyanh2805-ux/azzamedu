# Desk Readiness Checklist

**Alpha Elite · Apprentice M1L3 · Education only**

Use before every session (demo or live). If any item fails, **do not trade** — fix or sit out.

---

## Environment

- [ ] Dedicated session window blocked on calendar
- [ ] Phone notifications off or DND
- [ ] Charts + journal + worksheets open
- [ ] Broker/platform logged in and stable

## Mental state

- [ ] Slept adequately — not trading on exhaustion
- [ ] No revenge mindset from prior loss
- [ ] Clear intention: follow SOP, not "make it back"

## Risk preparation

- [ ] Account equity noted
- [ ] 2% (or your chosen cap) dollar amount calculated
- [ ] Daily max loss limit defined for today

## Plan

- [ ] Economic calendar checked for high-impact events
- [ ] At least one scenario written (if/then) — or conscious decision to observe only
- [ ] Pre-trade risk gate printed/available
