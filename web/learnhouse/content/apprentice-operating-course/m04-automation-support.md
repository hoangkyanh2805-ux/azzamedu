# M4 — Automation Support Literacy

**Module outcome:** Understand automation as support tooling — not passive income.

---

## M4L1 — Automation overview (education)

### Objective
Learn what automation tools can and cannot decide for you in a trading workflow.

### Video
`[PENDING: YouTube unlisted URL]` · Title: `AE Apprentice M4L1`

### Key points

- **Automation support** = alerts, scanners, or bots that **assist** execution you already planned — not replacements for risk discipline.
- Tools cannot remove market risk; they can only change *how fast* you act on **your** rules.
- Red-flag marketing: "earn while you sleep," "set and forget profits," "AI guaranteed entries."
- Alpha Elite DWY setup (optional service) helps **deploy** tools — it is not account management.
- You remain responsible for: size, stops, daily limits, and whether to trade at all.
- List tools you currently use; mark each as "decides for me" vs "supports my plan" — goal is zero in the first column.

## M4L2 — Tool checklist — manual decisions

### Objective
Complete the automation support checklist — every item you must still decide manually.

### Video
`[PENDING: YouTube unlisted URL]` · Title: `AE Apprentice M4L2`

### Key points

- Manual decisions that **never** get outsourced: risk %, max daily loss, sit-out rules, account funding level.
- Before enabling any bot or copier: written SOP, demo test, kill switch documented.
- If a tool fires without your pre-trade gate passing, treat it as a **bug in your system**, not a bonus trade.
- DWY Setup (`AE-DWY-001`) covers broker + bot **installation** — not ongoing trade management.
- Module 4 complete: checklist signed and filed with weekly review.
- No module in this course promises tool-driven returns.
