## M4L1 — 2% risk management framework

### Objective
Learn the definitions and educational application of the 2% risk cap — not as a profit formula.

### Video
`[PENDING]` · `AE Apprentice M4L1`

### Key points
- **2% rule (education):** risk no more than 2% of account equity on a single idea — adjust per your situation.
- Purpose: survive variance to execute your system long enough to learn.
- Risk = distance to invalidation × position size.
- Log account equity and 2% dollar amount privately in journal.
