Description

Have you ever missed opportunities, lost positions, or faced bad trading weeks despite a favorable market?




The issue isn’t your effort. It’s a lack of a solid system to truly understand the market.

You can’t identify what is happening on the charts.

You don’t know when a trend begins or when it ends to stop following it.

You struggle to locate key zones to wait for entry, set stop-loss, or determine take-profit levels.

You’re unable to pinpoint risk zones to control exposure effectively.

You cannot explain why the market moves up or down from specific price levels.

You’re unsure of where to start analyzing a chart.




If you cannot clearly explain the charts, misunderstand them, or fail to act strategically, you will continue facing losing weeks and stay stuck in trading.

In this course, you will learn how market structure solves critical trading challenges.


What You’ll Learn:

Market structure is a logical framework for analyzing any chart having charts to analyze from Forex, Crypto, Stocks, Indices, Metal.
It helps you to know detail about current trend, key zones (order block) that you can set entry, stop loss and take profit confidently.
With a strong logic inside, it helps you to explain why you win, lose and build a profitable and consistent trading system if you combine with other strategies : key level strategy, smart money concepts, ICT, supply demand, Ichimoku

In this course, you also learn detail price action behind a single candle, waves, zones to establish your cornerstone. And then, I will guide you in detail the rules to define single candles, pullback, break out, price action behind market structure to able to define an exactly market structure of any chart, even the chart has many gaps.

Combining Market Structure with Market Cycles, you will know when a trend is started, when it  is finished to have a big profit and forecast market picture easier, more exactly, optimize your reward per risk on each position.

Integrating Market Structure with Multiple Timeframes, you will know when you should control risk by move stop loss to entry or key level to reduce the risk because market comes to a risk zone.
You also know :
- Why market is going up from there. 
- Why market moved down like that.
- Is there any risk ahead of your position ? 
If so, your win rate will be increased, reward per risk on each trade will be optimized more.

All market structure, market cycle and multiple timeframe are guided in detail on this course.

Why should you join this course?
Market Structure not only helps you understand the market but also provides you with a solid foundation to:

Increase your win rate.

Optimize trading performance

Minimize unnecessary losses.

Build a systematic and logical trading strategy.

However, success doesn’t come easily. To truly master Market Structure, you need to understand even the smallest details from the story behind a single candlestick, the price action of candlestick groups, to evaluating the actual strength of a Marubozu candle.


This is why this course spans 18 hours of detailed content, ensuring that no critical aspect is overlooked.

This posts will be long, but if you spend time to read. It definitely help you.

------------------------ My trading journey ------------------------

There're somes sharing that haunted in my head every night.

We were NOT born with "a SILVER Spoon in mouth".

" If your 20 years old is poor, That's Normal.
If your 27 years old is still poor, I can sympathize;
If your 30 years old is still poor, you're really incompetent."

We cannot choose where will we born ?
But we can choose where we will die and how will we live.

So I decided to find a way for my life when I am learning in the top University of my country.

Trading journey really start after I joined a meeting about Crypto on 2015.

The reason why I joined that meeting even I have never heard about it before because I want to find the ROAD for my LIFE. I cannot work as officier 8-5 with 500$ per month That's quite high salary for an engineer on my country that time.

I feel that it is so terrible LIFE if I just live like that. A normal car on my country worth is 50,000$ at least.
A normal house is around 150,000$. Totally, 200,000$ and I need to works 400 YEARS.
That thoughts gave me the power to EXIT and find the new ROAD. I learnt so hard and work so hard.

I read so many books about trading that I don't even remember the numbers now : Price action (AL Boooks), RSI, VSA, MACD, Elliot,Wyckoff, Forex 100, Forex 101, Trend line, Support resistance, Somes books of Jess livemore, Mark minervini , William o'neil, ..... so many courses as well,

I wish I can earn money that equal to my salary before only (500$) but I have more time.
It means that I WIN about times to have more chance to develop.

I tried and tried and tried again so many years but I all FAILED . I cannot figure out the reason why, I just know I am very near my target but I cannot reach to it even 1 time.
Luckily, I never give up.... I came to the DEAD CYCLE that I shared you before.

Deadcycle mean that I just SEARCH new strategy --> Try --> Fail --> Search again Until I realize a problem that I still stand up at the START POINT after many years, I feel so terrible.

So stressful. Even 2:00 AM,  I still works to find my problem. I feel that " Profit is very near, try hard, just a little" . But, it has never came.

------------------------ "My turning point" ------------------------

Until one day, that's around 2018 Dec. I meet a trader in an other Meeting who changed my life completely.
He taught me about Key level strategy. It is very simple about key level and trend and entry with Confluence trend. I said" WOW. My holy grail here".
I see the logical, I see the results.

But when I practice after 4 months, results are just break even. Sometimes I have profit , sometimes I lost but If I got a losing streak, I need 2 months to pull back the performance. I drop to hole one more time. I feel so stressful again, but.

I still trust this strategy, I don't change it because my 1 day mentor manage 1m$ that time with really nice profit with strategy.


I think here is the TURNING POINT that help me have big improvement like today. I don't change the strategy and avoid the DEAD CYCLE to start again one more time.

I TRUST IT and decide to DEVELOP it by backtesting. I spend 2 months to practice to define correctly market structure with hundred of cases with pullback. Give out the final standard for PULLBACK, BREAK OUT.
And then, Market cycle, multiple timeframe, Unconfluence strategy, Smart Money Concepts came with me after back-testing thousands time and apply them to live trading to confirm again. They worked for me. I really found my own system from the cornerstone that one-day-mentor gave me.

I developed a strategy from very basic knowledge to the full view with market - My NCI trading system was borned. I call it as New Concept Intergration because I want to respect my one-day-member even a small sharing but he helped me to save a lot of time and effort on this career.

We all need a mentor to guide us to see the LIGHT.

I earned profit with it at the first time. The 2nd time and 3rd time that I can WITHDRAWN the money.

I scream " I can do it, I finally do it" even it just a little money  at the first time.

Now, I manage big big account and continue expanding my capital more with bigger profit but I still really respect the support from mentor and my effort in the past.

Everyone must do better than me !! I am here to help you save time and money than me before.

Never give up - Key mindset to be success

Trading is a PROCESS , It takes time to go to the TOP.
If I give up, I still a normal Engineer with salary 500$ per month.
