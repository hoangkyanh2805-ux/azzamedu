## FAQ additions (curriculum-specific)

**Do I need prior trading experience?**  
Basic familiarity with charts and market structure helps. The Gameplan covers foundations; Apprentice installs the operating protocol. Not suitable for complete beginners who skip risk education.

**Will you tell me exactly when to buy or sell?**  
**No.** You receive **structured trade ideas and frameworks for education** inside SOPs — not a signal service. You own every execution decision.

**Is live trading included as proof?**  
Walkthroughs are **educational case studies** — how structure, SOPs, and review discipline are applied. They are not promises of future results.

**How long to complete Apprentice?**  
~2–3 hours of video plus worksheet practice. We recommend applying the 7-day Gameplan sprint before Module 3.
