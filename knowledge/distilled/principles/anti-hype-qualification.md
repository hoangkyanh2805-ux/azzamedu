# Principle — Anti-Hype Qualification

## Mechanism
Premium education funnels convert better when copy *repels* wrong-fit buyers (signal chasers, passive-income seekers) before opt-in or checkout.

## Application (Alpha Elite)
- Lead with **operating system**, not alerts or wins
- Use calm, precise language — no urgency theater
- Disclaim risk **above the fold**, not buried
- Social proof = discipline stories, not P&L screenshots

## Copy tone
| Avoid (hype) | Use (system-first) |
|--------------|-------------------|
| "Kiếm tiền ngay" | "Vận hành có cấu trúc" |
| "Tín hiệu hot" | "Trade ideas có khuôn khổ" |
| "Bot print money" | "Automation support" |
| "Join free signals" | "Private operating system" |

## Reuse
Any membership, trading education, or high-ticket coaching where compliance and LTV matter more than top-of-funnel vanity metrics.
