## Positioning compliance

### NEVER publish

- [ ] Guaranteed profit, win rate, or monthly return %
- [ ] “Risk-free” trading or “can’t lose” framing
- [ ] Bot creates passive income / “earn while you sleep”
- [ ] Position as **signal group**, alert channel, or “free XAUUSD signals”
- [ ] Imply XAUUSD ideas = guaranteed gold profits
- [ ] Fake scarcity (countdown, seat count) unless verifiable
- [ ] P&L screenshots as promise or typical result
- [ ] Unlicensed advice (“buy gold now”)

### ALWAYS include

- [ ] **Operating system** framing (not signal group)
- [ ] Approved phrase: *education, trade ideas, automation support, SOPs, community support*
- [ ] **2% risk rule** as educational framework (not outcome guarantee)
- [ ] Trading **risk disclaimer** on: landing footer, PDF, emails, checkout, course videos, Telegram pin
- [ ] XAUUSD content framed as **structured trade ideas (education)** within SOP context
